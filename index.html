My Inspiration


Sunil Gavaskar


Sunil Manohar Gavaskar is an Indian cricket commentator and former cricketer representing India and Bombay from the early 1970s to the late 1980s. Widely regarded as one of the greatest batsmen and one of the best opening batsmen in Test cricket history, Gavaskar set world records during his career for the most Test runs and most Test centuries scored by any batsman.He held the record of 34 Test centuries for almost two decades before it was broken by Sachin Tendulkar in December 2005.
